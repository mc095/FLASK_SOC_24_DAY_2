About Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of nine players each. It is one of the most popular sports in the world, with a rich history and a huge following in the United States, Japan, and Latin America.

The Sluggers

The Sluggers are a fictional baseball team known for their powerful hitting and strong team spirit. They have won several championships and have a loyal fan base.

Team Members

  • John "The Hammer" Harris - First Baseman
  • Maria "The Speedster" Rodriguez - Center Fielder
  • David "The Ace" Lee - Pitcher
  • Emily "The Slugger" Patel - Third Baseman
